🐶 Happy Dog Care Guide

Loyal friends deserve the best care 🐾🐕

🌍Habitat

  • Dogs live with humans — houses, apartments, farms, or small spaces with daily exercise.
  • Must have clean, dry, and safe space — indoors or sheltered outdoor area.
  • Keep away from extreme cold or heat.

🍽️Food

  • High-quality dog food — dry kibble or canned (based on age and size).
  • Safe foods: Cooked meat (chicken, beef), vegetables (carrots, peas), rice, some fruits (apples, bananas).
  • Never give: chocolate, onions, garlic, grapes, alcohol, or cooked bones.

🌡️Temperature

  • Ideal range: 18°C – 26°C (64°F – 79°F).
  • In cold: Small dogs may need jackets or blankets.
  • In heat: Provide shade, cool water, avoid hot pavement walks.

💧Daily Care

  • Always provide fresh, clean water.
  • Exercise: At least 1 walk daily (30 minutes to 2 hours depending on breed).
  • Grooming: Brush regularly, bathe monthly, clean ears/eyes weekly, trim nails every 3–4 weeks.
  • Teeth: Brush 2–3 times a week or use dental chews.

🩺Health Tips

  • Vaccines: Rabies, distemper, parvovirus — as advised by vet.
  • Deworm every 3–6 months.
  • Use flea/tick prevention monthly (pills, collars).
  • Vet check-up once per year minimum.
  • Spay/neuter recommended if not breeding.

🎓Training

  • Basic commands: “Sit,” “Stay,” “Come,” “Down,” “Leave it.”
  • Short sessions daily (5–15 minutes).
  • Reward with treats, toys, praise.
  • Socialize early: People, animals, places.
  • Be patient — avoid yelling, use gentle correction.
