- Wild Habitat (Natural Fox):

- Domestic Habitat (Pet Fox):
- Requires a very large, secure outdoor enclosure (at least 200 sq. ft or more).

- Indoor Access:

- Legal Status:
- Keeping a fox as a pet is illegal or restricted in many countries or states. Always check local laws before considering one.
