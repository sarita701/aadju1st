🐤 Bird Information Guide (Pet Birds)


🌍 Habitat

  • Spacious, clean cage with perches, toys, and space to fly.
  • Place cage in calm area with natural light (no direct sun).
  • Avoid kitchen fumes and drafts.

🍽️ Food

  • Mix of seeds, pellets, fruits, and veggies.
  • Safe foods: apple (no seeds), spinach, carrots, rice.
  • Avoid: chocolate, avocado, garlic, caffeine.




🌡️ Temperature

  • Ideal: 18°C – 26°C.
  • Keep away from cold drafts.
  • Provide water and ventilation in hot weather.




💧 Daily Care

  • Fresh water daily.
  • Clean cage weekly and bowls daily.
  • Offer shallow bath or misting.
  • Birds need social time daily.




🩺 Health Tips

  • Visit avian vet yearly.
  • Watch for signs of illness (sleepy, no appetite).
  • Provide beak/nail care tools like cuttlebone.
  • Use safe, chewable toys.




🎓 Training

  • Speak gently and offer treats to build trust.
  • Train "step up" command with rewards.
  • Use positive reinforcement, never punish.
  • Parrots can learn tricks with patience!
