🐱 Happy Cat Care Guide

Love, Feed & Protect your little tiger 🐈💖


🌍Habitat

  • Safe and quiet indoor environment with cozy resting spots.
  • Provide vertical space like shelves or cat trees.
  • Hideaways and private corners are important for comfort.

🍽️Food

  • High-quality wet or dry cat food with meat protein.
  • Occasional treats: cooked chicken, eggs (no salt).
  • Avoid milk, chocolate, garlic, and raw meat.

🌡️Temperature

  • Ideal: 20°C – 26°C (68°F – 78°F).
  • Keep warm bedding for cold nights.
  • Offer cool spots and water in hot weather.

💧Daily Care

  • Fresh water every day (consider fountains).
  • Clean litter box daily and change weekly.
  • Brush your cat weekly (more if long-haired).
  • Playtime is essential — use toys & scratchers.

🩺Health Tips

  • Visit the vet once a year, or more often if sick.
  • Vaccines, flea control & deworming are essential.
  • Spay/neuter for health and behavior benefits.
  • Watch for changes in appetite, energy, or behavior.

🎓Training

  • Use a clean, quiet place for litter training.
  • Encourage scratching on posts, not furniture.
  • Some cats can learn simple commands with treats.
  • Be gentle, never punish — cats love routine and patience.
